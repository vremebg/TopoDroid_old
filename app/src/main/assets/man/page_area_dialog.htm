Drawing area

This dialog is opened when you select a point of the border of an area (in editing mode of the Sketch window), and tap the Edit button.

You can change the type of area, by selecting it from the scroll down list.

Area properties:

  • visibility of the area border. This has no effect on the way TopoDroid displays the area, but if used by Therion when the survey map is generated.

You can also reduce the nunmber of points.
